WHO I AM — a short story

I'm Chen — a photographer and videographer based in Be'er Sheva, with a passport that doesn't sit still. My week is split between the kitchens, dance floors, studios, and racetracks of Israel — and the months a year I spend somewhere I don't speak the language.

Plates, faces, motion, light. I shoot what'll outlive the moment. Selected work: brand films for Chicode and Viacci · restaurant content for Bar Giora & Saba Edward · the Petzael × Sparco track-day aftermovie · a music video for Maya Singer · a long-form Asia documentary still in the edit.

BEHIND THE SCENES

Every photo on this site has a story behind it — the 4am hike for one minute of golden hour, the chef who wouldn't stop refilling the plate, the bartender who set the bar on fire on cue, the corner of a Mongolian field that worked because we waited an hour for the cloud to move. The behind-the-scenes is the part I keep for the long cold nights of editing.
